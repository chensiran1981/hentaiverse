每当进入战场时自动开始战斗
战士参数设置：
血线低于数值时施放或使用:
healthGem cure healthPotion
regen healthDraught

法力低于数值时使用:
manaGem manaPortion manaDraught

灵力低于数值时使用:
spiritDraught spiritGem spiritPotion

获得Channeling，且BUFF剩余回合低于数值时施放:
regen heartSeeker 其他

怒气达到数值时切换Spirit姿态:
overcharge

自动保持以下BUFF:
keepHeartSeeker keepRegen keepHaste
keepProtection keepShadowVeil keepSpiritShield
keepSparkOfLife
